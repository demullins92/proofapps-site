Delete Your Account

TipTrackr — FoH Tip Tracker

⚠️ Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. All of your shift data, earnings history, and venue settings will be permanently removed.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open TipTrackr on your device
  2. Tap the Settings tab at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Need Help?

If you're unable to access the app or need assistance deleting your account, contact us directly and we'll remove your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Email: support@proofapps.co

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

• Your email address and profile information
• All logged shifts and tip records
• Earnings history and summaries
• Venue and tipout template settings
• Streak and milestone data
